Protocol guide tier system
T0 — Entry Modal
Multi-step progressive onboarding modals shown on launch day. Orient users to the new navigation and prompt them to begin a guided tour. Two variants: Full Org Manager and Role Manager.
T1 — Platform Orientation
High-level platform orientation surfaced from the top navigation. Activated programmatically. Two variants: Organization Manager and Role Manager.
T2 — Area Tour
Multi-step tours of a specific product area — Org Tour and Opp Level Overview. Longer walkthroughs (11+ views/user) designed to familiarize users with a functional section of VMS.
T3 — Feature Walkthrough
Deep, feature-specific walkthroughs (Volunteer List, Schedule, Roles, Tags, Signup Workflow, Qualifications, Template Reports, Email Automations, Importing, Attendance). Available at Org and Opp levels.

Interpretation & recommendations
What's working

The T2 Org Tour (402 viewers, 7.5% completion, 8.5% dismissal, 11.2 VPU) and T2 Opp Level Overview (154 viewers, 9.9% completion, 8.3 VPU) are the best-performing guides in the protocol. The T1 Platform Orientation for Org Managers (101 viewers, 10.2% completion, only 7.6% dismissal) also shows healthy engagement. Once users reach the walkthrough layer, the protocol is working.
Priority fix: T0 entry modals

The four T0 and Day Of modals account for the majority of all guide impressions, yet carry 0% completion and 29–70% dismissal rates. The funnel breaks before users ever reach T1/T2. Consider revising the framing, timing, or visual design of these entry modals to reduce immediate closure and improve take-up of the "Take the Tour" CTA.
