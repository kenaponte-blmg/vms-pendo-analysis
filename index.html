Interpretation & watch items
Positive signals

Visitor count is 14% above baseline. Stickiness is up 48% — the strongest signal that protocols are driving return behavior. The T1/T2 walkthroughs (Org Tour, Opp Level Overview, Platform Orientation) perform well for users who reach them. The new Opportunity Dashboard was immediately adopted as the primary navigation hub.
Watch items

Adoption (−18%) and Growth (−32%) are below baseline — monitor over the next 2–4 weeks. T0 modal dismissal rates of 62–70% mean the entry point is not converting users into the guided experience. Top-nav icon usage dropped sharply — confirm this is intentional routing, not users failing to find key features. NPS of −50 warrants attention as volume grows.
